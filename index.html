Reflection on AI-Assisted Programming

AI has lowered work barriers and boosted productivity greatly. It makes daily life easier and drives economic changes. Yet, it risks fostering lazy thinking—over-reliance may weaken our ability to think and solve problems independently.

I see AI as a tool, not a substitute for my mind. I’ll use it to handle simple tasks and focus on improving my own thinking and creativity. Only this way can we benefit from AI without losing our core strengths.
